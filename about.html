About me

I'm Kifilwe Jasmin Maroga, a creative and detail-oriented Front-end Developer and Virtual Assistant based in Gauteng, South Africa. I specialize in building visually stunning, user-friendly websites that combine sleek design with seamless functionality. Coding became my passion in 2023 when I joined the SheCodes program, and since then, I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, diving into the world of responsive web design. What sets me apart is my ability to think creatively while solving problems technically. Whether it's crafting a beautifull website or managing administrative tasks, I thrive on organization and efficiency. My virtual assistant experience has given me strong skills in task management, scheduling, communication, and support - allowing me to bring a unique blend of technical expertise and operational excellence to every project I work on. I'm constantly seeking opportunities to grow and challenge myself, both as a developer and an assistant. Beyond my professional life, I love exploring new books, watching movies and anime, and pushing myself creatively. I'm excited to continue expanding my skills, making meaningful contributions, and delivering value in every role I take on.
